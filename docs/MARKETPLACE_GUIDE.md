# Marketplace Guide

TeachX has two marketplace surfaces:

- Teacher Marketplace: students discover teachers, view public profiles, bookmark teachers, and send booking requests.
- Learning Marketplace: teachers publish resources and students discover, preview, bookmark, and download resources.

Payments, payouts, live classes, and realtime chat are intentionally provider-ready but not live payment features.

## Operational Checks

- Confirm published resources are visible to students.
- Confirm drafts remain teacher-owned.
- Confirm premium resources remain locked unless ownership exists.
- Review marketplace analytics from the Admin Growth OS.
