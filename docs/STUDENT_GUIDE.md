# Student Guide

The student workspace answers one question: what should I learn today?

## Main Areas

- Continue Learning
- Today's Classes
- Recorded Classes
- Study Materials
- Assignments
- Attendance
- Progress
- Announcements

Students can submit assignments and manage notes/bookmarks. Other classroom data is read-only.

## Launch Notes

- Use the Student workspace for learning progress, AI tutor flows, practice, bookmarks, downloads, and teacher discovery.
- Marketplace purchases and premium ownership are prepared in the Commerce OS, but live payment checkout should remain disabled until launch approval.
- AI responses are learning support and should be checked against class material and teacher guidance.
