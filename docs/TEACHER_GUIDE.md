# Teacher Guide

Teachers work from one place: My Classrooms.

## Daily Flow

1. Open Dashboard.
2. Review today's classes.
3. Open the classroom.
4. Take attendance.
5. Share materials, assignments, announcements, or recordings.

AI helper buttons are available inside the workspace. They can prepare lesson plans, improve instructions, summarize notes, and draft assignments when `OPENAI_API_KEY` is configured.

## Launch Notes

- Complete the teacher profile before sharing public marketplace links.
- Use AI Studio history, bookmarks, exports, and resources to keep generated teaching material organized.
- Publish only reviewed resources to the learning marketplace.
- Commerce, payouts, live video, and realtime messaging remain provider-ready placeholders until enabled by the platform team.
